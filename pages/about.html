About Us

Mission

Pasig Catholic College is dedicated to nurturing academic excellence, holistic development, and strong moral values in its students, preparing them to serve God and society responsibly.

Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for quality formation, innovative teaching, and a community of learners committed to lifelong growth and service.

Core Values

Faith, Integrity, Excellence, Discipline, and Service guide the character formation and academic journey of every PCC student, fostering responsible and ethical citizens.

History

Founded in 1948, Pasig Catholic College has continuously grown to provide quality education through various academic programs and holistic formation, impacting generations of students in Pasig City and beyond.
